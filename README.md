# Liveable AI 🏡✨

**Find Your Perfect Home Based on Life Quality**

Liveable AI is an AI-Based Livability Decision Support Platform that offers personalized Livability Scores. Traditional real estate platforms focus mostly on property price and investment value, ignoring crucial daily factors like neighborhood safety, noise levels, and overall comfort. Our platform addresses this by combining multi-source data and machine learning to help renters, homebuyers, and remote workers make confident, informed decisions about where to live.

## 🚀 The Problem We Solve
Choosing a home is one of life's biggest decisions, yet most people lack access to critical information about their future neighborhood's quality of life.
* **Hidden Environmental Risks:** Noise levels, air quality, and safety issues are often discovered only after moving in.
* **Price-Focused Platforms:** Traditional real estate sites focus on investment value, ignoring daily living comfort.
* **Time-Consuming Research:** Vital information is scattered across multiple sources and hard to interpret.

## ✨ Key Features
* **AI-Powered Analysis:** Machine learning models predict noise and crime trends based on temporal patterns throughout the day.
* **Safety Scoring:** Real-time safety metrics using official data from AFAD and local authorities to assess neighborhood security.
* **Temporal Neighborhood Analysis:** Unlike static tools, we show how noise and activity levels change from morning to night in your area.
* **Sociocultural Clustering:** Discover pet-friendly, family-oriented, or lifestyle-specific neighborhoods tailored to your needs.
* **Personalized Recommendations:** Get practical advice like soundproofing suggestions or better commute times to improve your quality of life.

## 📊 Data Sources
We integrate reliable, open datasets and official sources to ensure accurate scoring:
* **TÜİK** (Turkish Statistical Institute)
* **AFAD** (Disaster and Emergency Management Authority)
* **Municipal Data Portals** & Environmental Agencies

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
Liveable AI is built with a highly scalable infrastructure designed to process complex urban and spatial data.
* **Frontend:** React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, GSAP (for high-performance animations), and Vite.
* **Backend / Data Processing:** Python, Pandas, and GeoPandas for efficient data handling.
* **Database:** Cloud-hosted PostgreSQL with PostGIS for secure geographic and time-based data storage.
* **Machine Learning:** Custom predictive models for temporal noise and crime trends.
